Why Berlin?

Berlin, the city of history and culture

Berlin, Germany's vibrant capital, is a city where history comes alive at every corner. From the remnants of the Berlin Wall to the majestic Brandenburg Gate, Berlin tells the story of a nation's journey through triumph and tragedy. Once divided by the infamous Berlin Wall, the city has emerged as a symbol of unity, freedom, and resilience, making it one of Europe's most fascinating destinations.

Today's Berlin is a dynamic metropolis that seamlessly blends its rich historical heritage with cutting-edge modernity. You can explore world-class museums on Museum Island, enjoy the bustling atmosphere of traditional beer gardens, discover innovative street art in trendy neighborhoods like Kreuzberg, and experience the city's legendary nightlife. Berlin's unique character lies in its ability to honor its past while boldly embracing the future.

CAFÉS

My favorite cafés in Berlin

Café Einstein

A legendary Viennese-style coffeehouse in the heart of Berlin, famous for its traditional atmosphere, excellent coffee, and delicious Austrian pastries.

Address:

Kurfürstenstraße 58, 10785 Berlin

What I like about it

This historic café has been serving Berlin since 1996 and offers an authentic old-world coffeehouse experience with newspapers, chess games, and the best Sachertorte in the city!

Learn more

The Barn

Berlin's premier specialty coffee roastery and café, known for its exceptional single-origin beans and minimalist industrial design.

Address:

Schönhauser Allee 8, 10119 Berlin

What I like about it

This is where Berlin's third-wave coffee culture began! The baristas are true coffee artists, and you can watch them roast beans on-site. Perfect for coffee enthusiasts!

Learn more

Bonanza Coffee

A trendy specialty coffee shop in Kreuzberg that combines excellent coffee with a relaxed atmosphere and delicious homemade cakes and light meals.

Address:

Oderberger Str. 35, 10435 Berlin

What I like about it

Located in the hip Prenzlauer Berg district, this café has a wonderful community feel. Great for laptop work, meeting friends, and enjoying some of Berlin's best coffee!
